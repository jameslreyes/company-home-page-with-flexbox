About Us

Kenny's Kitchen was started in the bedroom of artist Kenny tha Kid when he was 24. He had a vision of a streamlined music recording company whose goal would be to provide the best sounding mixes and masters at the most affordable prices possible. Over the years, the team went from being just Kenny to an entire arsenal of engineers with one simple goal in mind: make it sound good.
